Founded in 2015 by Master Chef Hiroshi Tanaka, Ramen House brings the authentic taste of Japanese ramen to your neighborhood. With over 20 years of experience in Tokyo's finest ramen shops, Chef Tanaka crafts each bowl with traditional techniques and modern innovation.
